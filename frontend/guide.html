PCOS & Thyroid Health Guide

🏃 Exercise

Duration: 30–60 min/day

  • Strength training → 3–4 days/week
  • Cardio (walking/jogging/cycling) → daily
  • Yoga → helps stress & hormones
Why:

Improves insulin resistance & supports weight control

Simple Plan:

Morning: 20–30 min walk

Evening: Dumbbell / bodyweight workout

🥗 Diet

✅ What to Follow

  • High protein (eggs, paneer, dal)
  • Fiber-rich foods
  • Healthy fats (nuts, seeds)
  • Low GI foods

❌ What to Avoid

  • Sugar
  • Maida & bakery items
  • Junk food
  • Limit white rice
Example Day:

Breakfast: Oats / eggs

Lunch: Rice + dal + vegetables

Dinner: Light roti + sabzi

💊 Medication

  • Take thyroid medicine early morning
  • Empty stomach
  • Same time daily
⚠️ Skipping medication = no improvement

😴 Sleep

  • 7–8 hours daily
  • Fixed sleep schedule
  • Avoid late nights
Improves hormonal balance & reduces symptoms

🧘 Stress Management

  • Yoga / meditation (10–15 min)
  • Avoid stress triggers
High stress worsens PCOS

💧 Hydration

Drink 2.5–3 liters water daily

📊 Tracking

  • Period dates
  • Weight
  • Symptoms (hair fall, acne, mood)

📅 Weekly Habits

  • Weight check: once/week
  • Cheat meal: once/week
  • Doctor visit: every 3–6 months
